# PickleCounter Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026

PickleCounter is a pickleball scoring app. We respect your privacy and built the app to need none of your data.

## Information we collect

PickleCounter does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not require an account, does not connect to the internet, and does not include analytics or advertising.

## Data storage

Any game information you enter, such as scores, player names, and settings, stays only on your device inside the app. It is never transmitted anywhere. Deleting the app removes this information.

## Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.

## Third party services

PickleCounter does not use third party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
