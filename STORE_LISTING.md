## Description
PickleCounter is a fast, no nonsense scoreboard for pickleball.

Tap to score. PickleCounter handles the parts of the rules that are easy to lose track of, so you can focus on the game:

- The full score call: server score, receiver score, and server number
- Who is serving and which side the serve comes from
- Server 1 and server 2 rotation and side outs in doubles
- Singles and doubles modes
- Adjustable game target and an optional win by two
- Player names, so you always know who serves and who receives
- One tap undo for any mistake
- A clean, high contrast display you can read from across the court

No ads. No account. No internet needed. Your scores never leave your phone.

Grab your paddle and let PickleCounter keep score.
