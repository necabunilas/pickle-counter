Frequently asked

How do I score a point?

Tap the panel of the team that won the rally. Only the serving team can score, so tapping the receiving team records a side out instead.

What do the three numbers mean?

In doubles the score is called as server score, receiver score, and server number (1 or 2). Singles shows just server score and receiver score.

How do I undo a mistake?

Tap Undo. It steps back through every action, one tap at a time.

Can I change the target score or win by two?

Yes. Use the Game to field to set any target, and toggle Win by 2 on or off.

Does the app need the internet or an account?

No. PickleCounter works fully offline, has no account, and keeps everything on your device.
